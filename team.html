Meet the team

Ben Thompson

Technical Director

Ben started Majestic Motors in 2015 after a 15-year career in the automotive industry as a Design and Production Engineer, working for iconic brands such as Triumph Motorcycles, Aston Martin, Marcos, TVR and Caterham.

At Majestic Motors, Ben heads up the workshop side of the business, supporting technicians and overseeing quality. He also designs and engineers custom components, estimates jobs and assesses and diagnoses vehicles.

Ben has an eclectic collection of classic cars, most - truth be told - in need of full restoration. His favourite marque is Mercedes Benz - between them, he and his partner own a pair of SLs - one classic, one modern!

You can read more about Ben's experience here.

Chris Clarke

Commercial Director

Chris had a successful career building and developing software companies before launching Majestic Motorsport with Ben in 2017. Following a successful couple of years managing the race team, Chris became a partner in the restoration and storage businesses in early 2020.

Chris leads on sales, marketing, business development and customer services across all three businesses.

Chris has a soft spot for German and Italian sports cars, and owns an air-cooled Porsche and a 1972 Alfa Romeo GT Junior, which he as been slowly "improving" for about 15 years.

Adam

Technician

Adam joined Majestic Motors in 2018 as a Technician, focusing mainly on mechanical recomissioning, rebuilds and servicing.

Adam has a passion for Americana - his fantastically patinaed Chevvy side step pickup is currently making way for an incoming Impala.

Andy

Apprentice

Andy joined as an apprentice in early 2020 and is currently studying at the Heritage Skills Academy (based at Biscester Heritage). As well as helping out in the workshop, Andy is currently building one of our Citroen C1 race cars up from a bare shell.

As well as a Renault Clio Sport, Andy owns a 1975 Alfa GT Junior which he is slowly restoring as funds and time allow.

Lisa

Office Manager

Lisa joined Majestic Motors in 2017. She is responsible for all office administration, as well as looking after customers and helping the workshop by delivering to our specialist subcontractors and picking up parts.

Lisa is partial to a convertible and currently drives a Mazda MX-5 with an extremely fetching duck tail spoiler.

Paul

Senior Technicianr

Paul joined Majestic Motors in 2018 following a long career in bodywork and accident repair. He is a master fabricator and is generally involved in metalwork repair usually required in the earlier stages of restoration.

Paul restores his own vehicles in his spare time - he is currently completing his VW bay window camper - but his favourite marque is Alfa Romeo, owning both a Brera (daily driver) and a 105-series GTV.

Vic

Senior Technicianr

Vic is our longest serving member of staff, having joined near to the start of the business in 2015. A skilled welder and painter, he also performs mechanical work, so is a general all-rounder in the workshop.

Vic is a keen motorcyclist owning an original Honda Fireblade and Kawasaki Z1000SX. When he has a week off, it's not unusual for Vic to jump on a bike and ride through France to Italy or Germany!
