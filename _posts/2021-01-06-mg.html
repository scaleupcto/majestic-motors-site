Marque Focus: MG

Servicing, repair, recommissioning and restoration

At Majestic Motors we have extensive experience with all models of MG including MGA, MGB, MGC, Midget and even the pre-war models such as the J-Type.

We can offer simple servicing, through mechanical recomissioning or overhaul, to full body restoration and trim.

Below you'll find a small sample of the project work we have completed recently:

  • MG B Roadster bodywork repair
  • MG A Chassis repair
  • MG C Full mechanical recommissioning
  • MG J2 Full recommissioning and engine rebuild
  • MG B Coupe mechanical work and kingpin replacement

MG B Roaster bodywork repair and full respray

This B Roaster came to us as a tidy looking car with some rust bubbling in the rear arch area. Unfortunately it had been repaired poorly in the past and required whole new lower wing areas letting in as well as some work to the inner arches and sill ends. We also reshaped the rear bootlid for a better fit, made repair panels for the inner quarters (a previous owner had cut holes for speakers) and finished the car with a complete respray.

MG A Chassis repair

This MGA chassis had been repaired and patched over the years, and required replacement to both main chassis rails either side of the passenger compartment. The owner wanted to make sure it was straight and true before embarking on a home restoration.

We secured the chassis at the main hard points to our chassis table and measured to make sure it remained square and true whilst we cut out, remade and replaced sections of the rails.

This kind of work is impossible to do properly without use of the table to maintain the integrity of the chassis

MG C Coupe major mechanical, electrical recomissioning and trim

This MG C came to us having been hastly assembled elsewhere. We built a new wiring loom, reconditioned all the running gear including e-coating all the suspension and re-trimmed and sound-proofed the interior.

MG J2 Full recommissioning and engine rebuild

This J2 was restored many years ago and had been laid up by the owner due to a water leak from the block. We dismantled the engine and sent the block away for specialist repair. We are now rebuilding the engine to original specification, as well as attending to the usual recommissioning items when a car has been laid up for a period - brakes, fuel system, electrics.

MG B Coupe Mechanical work and kingpin replacement

This MG B had been subject to an engine rebuild elsewhere but was not running correctly and had not been used much in the last couple of years. The owner commissioned one of our engineer's inspection reports which lead to some recommendations including re-torquing the head, setting the valve clearances, installing electronic ignition and tuning the carbs.

With the engine running correctly we also fixed an issue with the driver's door handle and fuel gauge, and addressed worn front kingpins - a common MOT failure point on these cars.
