Morgan 4/4

Introduction: 1965 Morgan 4/4 Full Restoration

We are honoured to have been entrusted to restore this 1965 Morgan 4/4 by a local family, who have owned it for over 45 years. The brief for the project was to perform a full restoration whilst retaining as much of the original car as possible whilst making some safety, reliability and usability modifications to ensure the car can be enjoyed for years to come by the owner and her children.

Full storing coming very soon.
